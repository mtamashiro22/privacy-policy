Privacy Policy

Family Homework Assistant  |  Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Overview

Family Homework Assistant ("the Service") is a private, family-only SMS chatbot that helps pre-approved family members track school homework assignments, due dates, and reminders for school-age children. The Service operates on a private home server and is not open to the general public.

2. Who Can Use This Service

The Service is restricted exclusively to a small group of pre-approved family phone numbers. No member of the public can interact with or enroll in this Service. Access is manually granted and managed by the Service owner.

3. Information We Collect

The Service collects only the minimum information necessary to function:

  • Phone numbers – used solely to identify authorized family members and route SMS messages to the correct household.
  • Message content – inbound and outbound SMS messages (e.g., homework entries, due dates, reminders) are stored locally on a private home server to maintain assignment records.

We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, financial information, or any other personal data.

4. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used exclusively to:

  • Verify that a sender is an authorized family member
  • Log, track, and remind users of homework assignments and due dates
  • Send SMS replies and reminders via the Twilio messaging platform

Your information is never used for marketing, advertising, or any commercial purpose.

5. SMS Messaging & Opt-Out

By participating as an approved user of this Service, you consent to receive SMS messages related to homework tracking and reminders.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message from the Service. You will immediately stop receiving messages. To re-enable the Service, contact the Service owner directly.

Message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties for their own purposes.

The Service uses Twilio solely as an SMS delivery platform. Twilio processes outbound and inbound messages on our behalf. Twilio's privacy practices are governed by Twilio's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on a private, password-protected home server (Raspberry Pi). No data is stored in any public cloud service. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect stored information from unauthorized access.

8. Data Retention

Message and phone number records are retained only as long as needed to provide the homework tracking service. Records can be deleted upon request by contacting the Service owner.

9. Children's Privacy

While this Service is designed to assist school-age children with homework, it interacts exclusively with approved adult family members via SMS. The Service does not directly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any updates.

11. Contact

This is a private, family-operated service. For questions or to request data deletion, please contact the Service owner directly via SMS reply or through your existing family communication channels.
