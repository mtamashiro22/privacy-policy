SMS Opt-In & Consent

Family Homework Assistant  |  Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. How Users Are Enrolled

Family Homework Assistant is a private, invitation-only SMS service. No member of the general public can sign up or interact with this service. Enrollment works as follows:

  • A family member verbally requests access from the Service administrator (the household parent or guardian managing the service).
  • The family member provides their mobile phone number directly and in person to the administrator.
  • The administrator manually adds the phone number to the approved list on the home server.
  • The family member receives a welcome SMS confirming they have been enrolled and explaining how to opt out.
No phone number is ever enrolled without the direct, explicit, personal consent of the individual. All consent is obtained prior to any messages being sent.

2. What Users Consent To

By providing their phone number and requesting enrollment, users explicitly consent to:

  • Receiving SMS messages from Family Homework Assistant related to homework assignments, due dates, and reminders
  • The storage of their phone number on a private home server solely for the purpose of routing messages
  • Message frequency that varies based on homework activity and reminders set within the household

Message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

3. Opt-Out Instructions

Enrolled users may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message received from the Service. Upon receiving a STOP reply:

  • The user's number is immediately removed from the active messaging list
  • No further messages will be sent to that number
  • To re-enroll, the user must contact the Service administrator directly

Users may also reply HELP at any time to receive basic usage and opt-out information via SMS.

4. No Marketing or Third-Party Sharing

Consent to receive messages from Family Homework Assistant is never shared with, sold to, or transferred to any third party. Phone numbers collected are used solely to deliver the homework tracking service described in our Privacy Policy.

5. Contact

For questions about enrollment or to request removal from the service, contact the Service administrator directly via SMS reply or through your existing family communication channels.
